Desired Solution

For the 2022 CMS HCPCS Annual Update, DCH may submit procedures they want DXC to "mirror" or "mimic". This impact SURProfiler Case Type's definition. This change order is to add new procedure codes or diagnosis codes to the impacted SURProfiler Outpatient Case Types.


Business Impact

SURProfiler Outpatient Case Types will be updated accordingly based on CMS HCPCS Annual updates.


Technical Specifications

Add new procedure codes or diagnosis codes to the impacted SURProfiler Outpatient Case Type codes. Attached please find the spreadsheet "2022 HCPCS SUR Impacts.xls" for the detail information.
